Implimentation of all template files:

1. deliveredorder.ejs:
- This template is used to notify customers when their order has been delivered. It features a prominent header with your brand logo and a purple-magenta gradient background for strong brand identity.
- The body includes a personalized greeting, delivery details, and a summary of the ordered products (with images, titles, quantities, and prices).
- The footer contains copyright information and brand name, reinforcing professionalism.

2. pendingorder.ejs:
- This template informs customers about their pending orders. It uses the same brand colors and logo for consistency.
- The body provides a personalized message, order status, and a list of products in the order, each with an image, title, and price.
- The footer maintains brand presence and copyright.

3. promotion.ejs:
- This template is designed for product promotions and marketing campaigns. It uses the brand gradient and logo in the header.
- The body introduces featured products, each displayed with an image, name, description, and a 'Shop Now' button for easy access.
- The layout is visually appealing, with product cards and clear calls to action, ideal for driving engagement.
- The footer keeps the brand visible and professional.

4. welcome.ejs:
- This template welcomes new users to your platform. The header displays your logo and brand colors.
- The body contains a warm greeting, a brief introduction to your brand, and encouragement to explore products and offers.
- It also provides contact information for support and a friendly closing message.
- The footer includes copyright and brand name, ensuring a consistent look across all communications.

All templates use your logo at /assets/logo.jpg and a purple-magenta gradient background to reinforce your brand identity and create a visually appealing, professional experience for your users.
