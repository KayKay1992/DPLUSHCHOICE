Further explanation of each product controller:
 - createProduct: Handles adding a new product to the database, validating input and saving product details.
 - getProducts: Retrieves all products from the database, supporting listing and searching.
 - getProductById: Fetches a single product by its ID for detailed viewing.
 - updateProduct: Updates an existing product's information based on provided data and product ID.
 - deleteProduct: Removes a product from the database using its ID.
