## Wholesale Pricing Implementation Summary

### What Was Implemented:
I successfully implemented automated wholesale pricing functionality that activates when users select quantities meeting the minimum wholesale threshold. This feature dynamically adjusts pricing in real-time across the product details page and shopping cart.

### Key Changes Made:

#### 1. ProductDetails.jsx Updates:
- **Enhanced Price Display Logic**: Added helper functions `getCurrentPrice()` and `getOriginalPrice()` to handle wholesale vs. regular pricing display
- **Wholesale Status Detection**: Implemented `isWholesaleActive` check that monitors quantity against `product.wholesaleMinimumQuantity`
- **Visual Indicators**: Added wholesale pricing badges and crossed-out regular prices when wholesale pricing is active
- **Cart Integration**: Updated `handleAddToCart()` to use correct pricing based on wholesale status and include wholesale flags

#### 2. cartRedux.js Updates:
- **Enhanced Cart Item Storage**: Modified `addProduct` reducer to store wholesale pricing data (`wholesalePrice`, `wholesaleMinimumQuantity`, `discountPrice`, `originalPrice`) with each cart item
- **Dynamic Price Recalculation**: Updated both `addProduct` and `updateProductQuantity` reducers to recalculate prices in real-time when quantities change
- **Wholesale Activation Logic**: Added logic to automatically switch between regular and wholesale prices based on quantity thresholds
- **Persistent Wholesale Flags**: Cart items now track `isWholesale` status for proper display and calculations

#### 3. Cart.jsx (Future Enhancement):
- The cart component is ready to display wholesale pricing indicators once the backend provides the necessary wholesale data fields
